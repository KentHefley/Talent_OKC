Do you have what it takes to be the next big superstar? Brought to you by TALENT-X Entertainment, TALENT OKC is Oklahoma's own vocal talent show audition. Since 2010, TALENT OKC has showcased some of the most talented artists and entertainers from around the state. Artists like Hinder, Starlight Mints and The All American Rejects were discovered on TALENT OKC.
